Restaurant Registration Process

Step by step guide to register your Restaurant on our site

1. Register Yourself

Open foodnerd.pk/business

Enter your

• Email address and

• Password to register with us.

Enter the varification code sent on your email and phone number.

Become a partner with Foodnerd POS by providing your basic details & Choose our affordable subscription option.

2. Create Location

Online presence of your restaurant or café for your customers worldwide is one click away with Foodnerd POS. Add following details and create your restaurant:

• Hotel/Restaurant name

• Address

• Other essential details

And the location for your restaurant or cafe is created!



3. Add Menu

Add menu of your restaurant/café by entering following details:

• Menu title

• Dishes names

• Portion size in options

• Sides with main course

Make your complete menu available for sale online !!

4. Shipping and Payments Configuration

Set your shipping polygons to the areas in which you offer delivery and provide bank account details for setting up your payment account. You can define different charges and available area range for your customers.

Foodnerd POS supports multiple modes of payments such as Cash, Credit Cards & Digital Payments.

5. Go LIVE

You are all set! Just click save, and you are live!
